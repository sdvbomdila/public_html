School Building

The school is spread over an area of 8.41 acres that is 34,034.059 square meters of land amidst the captive serene and pollution-free natural beauty with a picturesque landscape in its vicinity. It has separate buildings/rooms for each classrooms, hostel boarders, kitchen, dining hall, recreation room, teaching staff quarters and non-teaching staff quarters with 24x7 electricity, water and Wi-Fi service.
