Social Service

School is said to be a gateway to adult opportunities for the students and a resource for parents and communities. School has a special responsibility to take a leadership role in environmental awareness and action. These environmental principles recognize interdependence, the right to a healthy environment and that everything we do has an effect. Our commitment to environmental principles is displayed through daily activities, decision-making and planning for the future. At SDV we provide leadership for system- and school-based programs, projects and events and also encourage community-based events to advance environmental awareness, education and care.

Our school believes in the concept of Clean India Clean School‚Äù and actively participates in all kind of cleanliness drive whether it is physical cleanliness of the self or environmental cleanliness of the school and nearby community.

Environmental Initiative

Environmental education connects us to the world around us, teaching us about both natural and manmade environments. It raises awareness of issues impacting the environment upon which we all depend, as well as actions we can take to improve and sustain it

Whether we bring nature into the classroom, take students outside to learn, our school has always been the first to enter any cleanliness movements around the city. We offer weekly and monthly Swachhata Abhiyan from our side not only to keep the school surroundings clean but also to take initiatives for the town as well.
