Hostel

Our school offers hostel facilities that are designed to provide a ragging free ‘home away from home’ atmosphere to the children from class I to class X. Parenting Teams are assigned to every group of students to take care of their academic, physical and psychological needs. Special attention is provided to the junior boarders, who are under the motherly care of trained lady attendants throughout the day and night. The school takes responsibility to safeguard the health of its students. The school provide a second home to our boarders with such facilities as :

  • Rooms are available in our hostel with beds designed to accommodate each age group students.

  • Nutritious and delicious food is served in a clean and hygienic Dinning Hall. Care is taken to ensure that the menu offers a wide variety of interesting choices to keep the students love their diet.

  • Games and Sports Facilities- Available

  • Laundry Service – Available .

  • Special coaching for all the boarders - Available.

  • The parents can call the students on every Sunday between 9:00 am. To 9:00 p.m.

  • Phone facilities available to communicate with the parents.

Allotment of Rooms



  • Hostel accommodation is allotted purely at the discretion of the school authority, warden and on condition that the student agrees to abide by all the rules and regulations of the hostel.

  • Students need to avoid singing aloud, shouting or making all types of noises which are likely to distract the attention of those who may be studying in their rooms.

  • The hostel facility will only be provided to the student after personal interview. The conduct and behavior will be closely observed for continuing his admission in the hostel.
